ABOUT US

At Main Event Barbershop, we pride ourselves in tradition. We specialize in services from old school razor shaves to more trending styles, such as fades, tapers, faux hawks, and more.

Main Event was established in 2006 and was named after the sport of boxing. Aside from our customers enjoying the history of the sport through our shop displays, we make sure each client feels like they are part of the “Main Event.” On top of giving you a fresh cut, customer service and cleanliness is our #1 priority.
